A QUESTION YOU MIGHT ASK

Check-in and check-out times?

Normal check-in from 3pm onwards. Check-out before 11am. You can stay in the resort or leave luggage safely if you arrive earlier.

Pick up, drop off?

We automatically offer free pick up or drop off from/to Siquijor Port and Airport (10-15 minutes from the resort) and a discounted pick up or drop off from/to Larena Port (45 minutes) for guests who book directly with us on our own website. Please call/text us a day or two in advance to confirm pick up time and place on our mobile numbers: 0908 1656 277 (Smart) or 0977 9244 677 (Globe). You can WhatsApp us on the Globe number and there is free wi-fi in most ferry terminals.

Late check-in?

We leave keys with the night guard for guests arriving after the Reception closes at 10PM. No problem.

Direct booking?

Direct booking means booking directly with us and not via the online travel agencies (OTAs) like Agoda, Booking.com, Expedia etc. who keep at least 17-20% of the room price you pay. We can price match when you book directly with us and include add-ons/extras to give you a better overall deal. See our Promotions.

Dive & snorkel?

Siquijor diving and snorkelling is not (yet) well known but is very, very good. Turtles are common and divers regularly see sharks, rays, and larger pelagics. Corals are still in good condition in many places and the area is good for macro-photography. There are several dive shops and we can help book.

What are the beds like?

Our mattresses are comfortable, and we provide European-style duvets rather than blankets.

Cleaning of rooms?

Daily cleaning unless you ask for less. Careful cleaning between guests and airing of rooms.

Clean property?

We keep the resort, rooms, pool and beach clean and tidy. During the July-November monsoon season the onshore winds carry sea grass and plastic. We clean our beach section regularly, and sometimes guests volunteer to help out and do their bit for the local environment

Car parking?

We offer free, safe parking inside the resort.

Pets?

Pets are not allowed unless special cases and specifically agree with us beforehand.

Good for families?

We consider ourselves a safe and family-friendly resort (we are parents ourselves as are most of our staff).

Staff?

Our staff are from mostly from lain batangas. Some have formal qualifications. All have a great attitude towards hospitality and serving others.

Languages?

Our staff speak Tagalog and English.

Safety/security?

Hipnautic Beach Resort is generally safe with very little crime. We employ guards, but this is more to help monitor arrivals and departures and perform Covid-19 checks. We use cctv to monitor the resort grounds.

Bank transfers?

We accept bank transfers. Please email us for details.

Swimming?

The water is shallow and Solangon beach is generally not great for swimming. Around high tide you can take our free kayaks to the edge of the reef for swimming and snorkelling in clear water with some corals. And there are good swimming beaches nearby (e.g. Paliton Beach).

The beach?

Our Hipnautic beach is the longest white sand beach on Siquijor Island with great sunsets and not crowded. Around July-November the winds carry harmless seagrass and plastic on to the beach. We keep our beach clean.

Non-smoking?

All rooms and the restaurant are non-smoking areas. Smoking is allowed outside. The local government implements a non-smoking policy for all public areas.

Toiletries?

We provide free soap, shampoo and conditioner in refillable bottles. Please bring your own toothbrush and toothpaste. We minimise single use plastics.

Power supply?

We have our own back-up generator in case of power cuts/power shortages, for the whole resort including air-conditioners.

Eco-certification?

We minimise single use plastics, provide refillable water and shampoo + conditioner bottles, encourage water saving, use low-energy light bulbs, recycle organic waste, do waste separation, provide our own safe and pure groundwater from one of our organic farms. We may introduce solar power in future.
