>Cancelation, Adjustment and Refund Policy

For those who wish to cancel and ask for refund, we are informing you that we will follow our cancellation and adjustment policy. We advise you to rebook, without charge, during this pandemic period. We may also refund due to some considerations. 

1. The guests understands and must agree with the booking cancelation, adjustment and refund policy upon booking.

2. Any cancellation of guest reservation must be requested by writing to the email address Hipnauticbeachresort@gmail.com least 7 days before the date of arrival at the hotel, providing all the details of the reservation. After this period has expired, no refund will be made to the amounts charged on the card provided by the customer or guest.

3. Regardless of the nights booked, if the change or cancellation is not made in the above-mentioned time and form, or in case the customer or guest does not appear on the date of entry, no refund will be made of the amount paid by the guest.

4. Any adjustment or rescheduling of guest reservation date must be requested by writing to the email address Hipnauticbeachresort@gmail.com least 7 days before the date of arrival at the hotel, providing all the details of the reservation. The requested date is subject to accommodation availability except for peak season so further details must be discussed thoroughly.

5. The refund process may take some time and will be discussed directly with the resort management through email.

Cancellation must be informed thru email. Cancellation made:

  • Less than 24 hours after booking –100% of the payment will be refunded
  • Less than 15 days prior to arrival date – 100% payment is forfeited
  • Less than 30 days prior to arrival date – 50% payment is forfeited
  • 31-45 days prior to arrival date – 25% of the total amount will be forfeited
  • More than 45 days prior to arrival date  – Full Payment Refund
